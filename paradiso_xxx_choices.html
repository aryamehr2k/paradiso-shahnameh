Speak with Beatrice

This piece talks to an AI that plays Beatrice, guiding you (Dante) through the vision of Paradiso XXX. To bring her to life it calls the Claude API from your browser, so it needs your own Anthropic API key.

⚠ Your key is stored only in this browser (localStorage) and sent directly to Anthropic — never to GitHub or anyone else. Because this is a static site, the key lives in your browser: use a personal key, not a shared/production one, and never commit it. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

No key? You can still play a short scripted version — close this and speak to her.
